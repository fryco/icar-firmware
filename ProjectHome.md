This is a open project, welcome to join in.
这是一个开源项目，欢迎你的加入。

这个项目是通过GSM模块使用GPRS，把汽车的一些信息上传到互联网上。
This project is dedicated to share vehicle information to internet by GSM module with GPRS

你可以通过互联网，把你汽车的数据，跟其它类型的汽车数据相比较，或对比你汽车的历史数据，
从而得出你汽车的健康状况，帮你省下不少紧急费用。
You can compare your vehicle data with others similar vehicle data in internet,
or your vehicle history data, to get a conclusion about your vehicle's status,
to save some emergency cost

例如汽车电池电压，如果你的电池电压比平均水平都要低，那是否需要检查一下电池？
避免要用车时却发动不起引擎？
For example, the car battery voltage, if your battery voltage is lower than average level, does it need to double check?
Avoid the engine cannot start when you need the vehicle.
